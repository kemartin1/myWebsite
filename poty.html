Player of the Year

Every season, the POTY trophy is awarded to the player judged most valuable in the Redford Premiere Hockey League. The selected individual is awarded based on league leading stats, as well as exceptional character on and off the ice. To date, the RPHL POTY, has been awarded 21 different times.

This year, we are pleased to announce the winner of the POTY is, Kyle Martin playing with the DH Flyers! Kyle lead the league in goals, assists, and points, and lead his team to their first ever championship. He is a proven leader who many others in the league look up to. He is currently in his 6th season.
